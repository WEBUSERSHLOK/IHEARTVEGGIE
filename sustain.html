Overview

Composting:
We use waste and leftovers from our restaurants for composting and recycling to be of better use for the soil, which increases the health and growth of the plant.
Zero Waste Policy:
Waste and leftovers are used for composting or secondary dishes.
Local Sourcing Policy:
All of our products come from local farms and initiatives. They strive to give us the best possible vegetation they can. They give us seasonal products and have harvest cycles to give us the freshest produce possible
Certified Green Restaurant
Awarded for reach high standards of using waste and efficiently using energy and water. Along with this, we are truthful about our food sourcing.
